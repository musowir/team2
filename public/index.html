Who we are

Team2 is a collaborative platform for communication, leadership and technology, and skills development. Our activities include storytelling, webinars, idea funding, public speaking, and technology projects. Team2 mentors help students in advanced studies, internships, and placements through a global consortium of companies, mentors, and experienced leaders.
